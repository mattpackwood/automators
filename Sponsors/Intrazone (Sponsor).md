# Intrazone
The Intrazone is a bi-weekly conversation and interview podcast hosted by the SharePoint team. The show highlights usage, adoption, and how SharePoint works for you. Hear from guest experts behind the scenes and out in the field. It's all about how SharePoint fits into your everyday work life – the goal being to more easily share and manage content, knowledge and applications, and to empower teamwork throughout your organisation with the technology you already have. Get your ears ready and subscribe today!

Transcripts are provided with each episode.
